Slow Cooker Texas Pulled Pork

Description:

The BEST Homemade Mac and Cheese of your LIFE. Outrageously cheesy, ultra creamy, and topped with a crunchy Panko-Parmesan topping, this mac and cheese recipe is most definitely a keeper.
I used three different cheese and a homemade cheese sauce to take this macaroni and cheese recipe over the top.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Elbow Macaroni
  • Panko Crumbs
  • Whole Milk
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Onion
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Pour oil into the slow cooker, then place the roast on top of the oil
  2. Add the remaining ingredients to the slow cooker
  3. Cover and cook until the pork shreds easily
  4. Shred the pork and return it to the slow cooker to combine it with the juices
  5. If you're making sandwiches, serve the pulled pork on buttered buns
